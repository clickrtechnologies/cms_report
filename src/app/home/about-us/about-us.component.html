About CMS CRBT Portal

The CMS CRBT Portal is a centralized digital platform built to manage the end-to-end lifecycle of Caller Ring Back Tone (CRBT) content. It connects CPs, Artists, MNOs, and Admins into a seamless workflow, boosting operational transparency and scalability.
